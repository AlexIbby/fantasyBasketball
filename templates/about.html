About Fantasy Basketball Stats

Welcome to Ibby's Fantasy Basketball Stats

This application helps fantasy basketball managers analyze their team's performance across different statistical categories. By connecting to your Yahoo Fantasy Basketball account, you can:

  • Compare Category Strengths - See how your team would perform against every team in your league, not just your weekly matchup.
  • Team Comparisons - View season totals or weekly averages for all teams in your league.
  • Roster Analysis - (Coming soon) Analyze the strengths and weaknesses of your roster.

Development

This application is built with Flask and connects to the Yahoo Fantasy Sports API. The frontend uses vanilla JavaScript for interactivity and responsive design.

The full source code is available on GitHub.
